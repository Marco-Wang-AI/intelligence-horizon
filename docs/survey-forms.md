# Survey Forms / 问卷内容

V1 uses two external forms:

These forms collect responses in their own dashboards. They do not automatically update the website. For launch, export CSV manually when needed; later, connect a database or scheduled import if public live stats become important.

## English Form: Almost O'Clock AGI / ASI Pulse

Description:

> Almost O'Clock is a playful AGI / ASI clock. This short survey asks when you think AGI and ASI might arrive, and what these words mean to you. Responses may be used in aggregate on the project website. No real name required.

Questions:

1. When do you think AGI arrives?
   - 2026 or earlier
   - 2027
   - 2028
   - 2029
   - 2030
   - 2031-2035
   - After 2035
   - Not sure

2. When do you think ASI / superintelligence arrives?
   - 2027 or earlier
   - 2028
   - 2029
   - 2030
   - 2031-2035
   - After 2035
   - Far away / not sure

3. Which field are you closest to?
   - AI / software / internet
   - Education
   - Design / media / content
   - Healthcare / biotech
   - Finance / operations / consulting
   - Student
   - Other

4. In your field, when can AI do roughly 90% of the work?
   - Already / 2026
   - 2027
   - 2028
   - 2029
   - 2030
   - 2031-2035
   - After 2035
   - Not sure

5. Optional: define AGI in one sentence.

6. Optional: define ASI in one sentence.

7. Optional: age range
   - Under 18
   - 18-24
   - 25-34
   - 35-44
   - 45+
   - Prefer not to say

8. Optional: what signal, launch, interview, or expert claim should move the clock?

Closing message:

> Thanks! This is not a prophecy, just a changing clock with receipts, guesses, and public intuition.
